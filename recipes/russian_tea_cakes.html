Russian Tea Cakes

Description

While these cookies may look hard, in reality they only take 5 ingredients! These cookies are a particular favorite of mine, espeically when drinking tea or coffee - due to their dry and crumbly texture it's advised to eat them with a drink.

Be mindful of the mess when baking or eating these cookies as powdered sugar is prone to get everywhere. And I do mean everywhere.

Ingredients

  • 1 c. unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 c. all-purpose flour
  • 6 Tbs powdered sugar
  • 1 c. finely chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 c. powdered sugar, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  3. Cream butter and vanilla together in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until smooth, 2 to 3 minutes
  4. Whisk flour and 6 tablespoons powdered sugae together in a seperate bowl. Add to the butter mixture and stir until just blended. Add walnuts and mix until incorporated; micture may be crumbly
  5. Taken small scoops of dough and roll between your hands into 1-inch balls. Place balls 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are just turning golden, about 12 minutes
  7. Remove from the oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool for 15 minutes
  8. Place remining 1/3 cup powdered sugae in a small bowl. Roll cooled cookies in the powdered sugar once or twice
  9. Enjoy
